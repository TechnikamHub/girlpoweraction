Our Programs

Transforming lives through education, skills, and mentorship

What We Offer

Sexual & Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR)

Comprehensive education on reproductive health, body changes, and healthy choices to empower girls with vital knowledge.

Menstrual health education, body awareness and puberty, reproductive rights advocacy, family planning education, prevention of gender-based violence, and healthy relationships training.

Tailoring & Livelihood Skills

Practical skills training for economic empowerment and financial independence, enabling girls to generate income.

Professional tailoring techniques, pattern making and design, business management skills, financial literacy, entrepreneurship training, and market access support.

Environmental Responsibility for Safe Reuse and Disposal of Sanitary Products

Teaching girls about environmental stewardship and sustainable practices for responsible waste management of menstrual hygiene products.

Environmental impact awareness, safe reuse techniques for cloth pads and menstrual cups, proper disposal methods, composting education, waste reduction strategies, and community clean-up initiatives.

Mentorship Program

Guidance and support from role models who help girls navigate challenges, build confidence, and achieve their goals.

One-on-one mentoring, leadership development, goal setting and planning, academic support, career guidance, and emotional support.

Menstruation Hygiene Management

Comprehensive education and provision of reusable sanitary products to ensure girls manage menstruation with dignity.

Hygiene education, reusable sanitary pad distribution, waste management practices, health and sanitation, addressing menstrual myths, and school attendance support.

Community Outreach & Support

Building community awareness and support systems to create lasting change beyond individual girls and their families.

Parent awareness programs, community workshops, school partnerships, advocacy campaigns, support groups, and family counseling.

Program Impact

Our integrated programs have created meaningful change in the lives of thousands of girls. Through combining education, skills training, mentorship, and practical support, we address the root causes of inequity and build pathways to empowerment.
