About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the impact we're making in the lives of refugee girls.

Our Story

Girl Power Action Initiative was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to restore dignity and happiness to refugee girls in Kalobeyei and Kakuma camps. Our founders witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by these young women, particularly concerning menstrual hygiene and reproductive health.

What began as a small initiative to provide reusable sanitary pads quickly evolved into a comprehensive program addressing sexual and reproductive health education, menstruation hygiene management, and providing mentorship opportunities.

Today, we continue to grow our impact, reaching more girls each year and expanding our programs to address their holistic needs. We believe that every girl deserves access to education, health resources, and the opportunity to reach her full potential.

"Every girl has the right to education, health, and dignity."

Our Mission

Girl Power Action Initiative is committed to promoting Sexual Reproductive Health Education (SRHE) and preventing Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV). Through educational training, mentorship, and livelihood programs, we empower women and girls to lead healthier, safer, and more empowered lives.

Our Vision

A future where every woman and girl in Africa lives and thrives with dignity, safety, and self-reliance.

Our Impact

In partnership with UNHCR, Cohere, and Amahoro Coalition, we have achieved: 4,218 girls trained on SRHR and GBV; 4,600 pads distributed to adolescent girls and reproductive women; 50+ women trained on tailoring and reusable pad production.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We believe in empowering girls with knowledge, skills, and confidence to take control of their lives and make informed decisions.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and ensure our programs are accessible to all girls, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Dignity

We respect the dignity and autonomy of every girl we work with, treating them with compassion and respect at all times.

Sustainability

We focus on long-term solutions that create lasting change in the lives of girls and their communities.

Accountability

We are committed to transparency and accountability in all our operations and the use of resources.

Collaboration

We work closely with partners, stakeholders, and communities to maximize our impact and reach.
