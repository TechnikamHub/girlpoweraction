Frequently Asked Questions

How can I support GPAI?

You can support us by donating, volunteering your time, or spreading the word about our mission. All forms of support make a real difference in the lives of the girls we serve.

What programs does GPAI offer?

We offer comprehensive programs including Sexual Reproductive Health Education, Menstruation Hygiene Management, Mentorship, Tailoring Skills, Environmental Responsibility for Safe Reuse and Disposal of Sanitary Products, and Community Outreach initiatives.

How can I become a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! Please contact us using the form above with your information and interests. We'll get back to you with available volunteer opportunities.

Where do your donations go?

All donations go directly to our programs and services. We maintain transparency in our financial operations and are committed to responsible use of resources.

How many girls have you helped?

In partnership with UNHCR, Cohere, and Amahoro Coalition, we have trained 4,218 girls on SRHR and GBV, distributed 4,600 pads to adolescent girls and reproductive women, and provided comprehensive support across the Kaloboyei and Kakuma refugee camps.
