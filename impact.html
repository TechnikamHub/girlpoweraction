Our Impact

Transforming lives and creating lasting change in refugee communities

Making a Real Difference

In partnership with UNHCR, Cohere, and Amahoro Coalition, we have achieved: 4,218 girls trained on SRHR and GBV; 4,600 pads distributed to adolescent girls and reproductive women; 50+ women trained on tailoring and reusable pad production.

Impact Areas

Health & Hygiene

We've provided comprehensive health education and practical solutions to improve menstrual hygiene management, ensuring girls can attend school and participate fully in their education.

  • Distributed 4,600 reusable sanitary pads
  • Trained girls on hygiene management
  • Reduced school absenteeism by 60%
  • Improved health awareness

Education & Skills

Through tailoring, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship training, we've equipped girls with practical skills for economic empowerment and future opportunities.

  • 50+ women trained in tailoring
  • 300+ girls completed digital skills
  • 100+ girls started small businesses
  • Average income increase of 40%

Mentorship & Support

Our mentorship program has provided guidance and emotional support to girls facing complex challenges, building their confidence and resilience.

  • 1,000+ girls in mentorship program
  • Monthly support sessions
  • 95% completion rate
  • Improved self-confidence scores

Community Change

We've worked with families and communities to shift attitudes and create supportive environments for girls' empowerment and well-being.

  • 500+ parent awareness sessions
  • 20+ community partnerships
  • Increased community support
  • Enhanced girl-friendly policies

Success Stories

From Struggle to Success

Amina, 16, was missing school 3 days every month due to menstrual challenges. After joining our menstrual hygiene program, she received reusable sanitary pads and comprehensive health education. She now attends school regularly and has become a peer educator, helping other girls navigate their health challenges.

Impact: 90% school attendance | Became a peer educator | Improved grades

Economic Empowerment

Sarah, 17, completed our tailoring skills program and started her own small sewing business. She now earns income to support her family and has become a trainer for other girls. Her confidence and future outlook have completely transformed.

Impact: Started her own business | Monthly income | Now training others
